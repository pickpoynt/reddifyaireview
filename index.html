Response Quality & Certiscore‚Ñ¢ Deep Dive

This is PingPolls' secret weapon. Certiscore‚Ñ¢ measures response authenticity using emotion detection, keypoint extraction, and preference intensity analysis.

How Certiscore Works (Plain English)

When someone answers your form, Certiscore analyzes:

  1. Emotion signals: Are they expressing joy, frustration, neutrality?
  2. Specificity: Vague answers ("It was fine") get lower scores than detailed ones
  3. Consistency: Do their ratings match their written feedback?
  4. Response time: Suspiciously fast submissions get flagged

Each response gets a Certiscore from 0-10, where:

  • 8-10: High confidence, authentic response
  • 5-7: Moderate confidence, may be surface-level
  • 0-4: Low confidence, likely being polite or rushing

"In my customer satisfaction survey, 23% of responses had Certiscores below 5‚Äîmeaning nearly 1 in 4 people weren't being fully honest. This explains why our NPS looked great but churn remained high. Certiscore gave us the real story."
